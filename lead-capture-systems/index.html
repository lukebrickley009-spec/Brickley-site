The stakes

Why lead capture matters

For a local business, a missed inquiry is missed revenue. Someone was ready to reach out — and something got in the way. Maybe the form was clunky, maybe the message went to a spam folder, maybe the reply came a day too late and they'd already booked someone else.

A lead capture system closes those gaps. It makes reaching out effortless, tells you the moment it happens, and helps you respond fast — because in local services, the first business to reply often wins the job.

The pieces

Inquiry forms

Good forms ask for just enough to start a conversation — name, contact, and what they need — without scaring people off with a wall of fields. They work smoothly on a phone, and they can be tailored to your business: a quote request, a booking inquiry, a "questions before I book" form, or a simple contact form.

  • ✓ Short, mobile-friendly forms that people actually finish
  • ✓ Quote requests, bookings, or general inquiries
  • ✓ Placed where visitors are ready to act
  • ✓ Spam-resistant so real leads stand out

The pieces

Instant notifications

The moment someone submits a form, you know. Notifications land in your inbox (or wherever you want them) with the details you need to reply quickly. No logging into a dashboard, no checking a form that only you remember exists — the lead comes to you.

  • ✓ Instant email alerts on every submission
  • ✓ All the details you need to respond, in one place
  • ✓ Reliable delivery so leads don't get lost
  • ✓ Routed to the right person, right away

The pieces

Follow-up workflow basics

Not every lead is ready to book on the first message — and slow or forgotten follow-up is where a lot of business quietly disappears. Simple, automatic follow-up keeps you in front of the lead: an instant "thanks, I got your message" reply, and gentle reminders so nothing falls through the cracks.

  • ✓ Automatic "we got your message" confirmations
  • ✓ Reminders so no inquiry is forgotten
  • ✓ Faster response times without extra effort
  • ✓ Built to fit how you already work
