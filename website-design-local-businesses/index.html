The reality

Why most local websites underperform

Plenty of local business websites look perfectly fine. The problem is what they ask the visitor to do — usually not much. There's no clear next step, the contact option is buried, and the page doesn't answer the questions a customer actually has before reaching out.

A website should do one job well: help the right visitor take the next step, whether that's an inquiry, a call, or a booking. When that path is clear and easy on a phone, more visitors become leads — without more traffic.
