The why

Why reviews matter

Before a new customer books a local business, they look for reassurance that other people have had a good experience. Reviews and testimonials are that reassurance. When they're visible and genuine, a hesitant visitor becomes a confident one.

The catch is that most businesses do great work but rarely capture proof of it — and when they do, it sits in an inbox or a screenshot folder instead of on the website where it builds trust. A simple system fixes that.

How it works

What the system does

Three simple steps, handled for you.

1

Collect

Happy customers submit their experience through a simple form — on their phone, in a minute.

2

Approve

You review each submission and decide what goes live. Nothing is published without your say-so.

3

Display

Approved testimonials appear automatically in a clean gallery on your website.

The details

Collecting testimonials

The easier it is to leave a testimonial, the more you'll get. Customers get a simple link to a short form — no account, no friction. You can send it after a job, add it to a thank-you email, or link it from a receipt. Every submission comes to you first.

  • ✓ Short, mobile-friendly submission form
  • ✓ Easy to share by link, email, or QR code
  • ✓ Instant notification when a testimonial arrives
  • ✓ You stay in control of what's published

The details

Displaying approved testimonials

Approved testimonials appear in a clean, on-brand gallery that fits your existing website — no clutter, no third-party widgets slowing your pages down. New visitors see real proof exactly where it helps them decide to reach out.

  • ✓ Clean gallery that matches your site
  • ✓ Approved reviews appear automatically
  • ✓ Fast-loading, mobile-friendly display
  • ✓ Placed where it builds the most trust
