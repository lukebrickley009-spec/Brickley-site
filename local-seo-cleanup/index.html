What it is

What local SEO cleanup means

Local SEO cleanup isn't about tricks or chasing algorithms. It's about making your website clear and organized enough that Google — and the person searching — can quickly understand who you are, what you do, and where you do it.

For a local business, most of the wins are practical: page titles that actually describe your services, descriptions that read well in search results, a sensible page structure, and consistent information across your website and Google Business Profile. Small fixes, done properly, help the right nearby customers find you.

Where I start

Common problems I look for

A quick review usually surfaces the same handful of issues on local business websites.

×

Generic or missing page titles that don't mention your service or town.

×

Weak or duplicate meta descriptions that hurt click-throughs from Google.

×

One long page instead of clear service and location pages.

×

No local keywords — your town and nearby areas aren't mentioned naturally.

×

Reviews are hard to find, so trust signals are hidden from visitors.

×

Inconsistent business info between the website and Google Business Profile.
